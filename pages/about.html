My name is Dana Alhawary. As a digital artist and illustrator working out of New Cairo, Egypt, I create and illustrate all kinds of cool stuff. As of right now, I am a student at Coventry University working for a Bachelor in Digital Media.

I'm open to new ideas and am eager to try anything that can help me improve as an artist.
